# Welcome to 244 Club

We are a vibrant community of Angolan students and professionals across the United Kingdom, united by our shared heritage and a passion for building bridges between Angola and the UK.

**244** is Angola's international dialling code — and it's become our symbol of connection, identity, and belonging.

## What We Do

We organise cultural events, networking opportunities, and social gatherings that celebrate Angolan culture while fostering academic and professional growth. From Luanda to London, from Benguela to Birmingham — wherever you are in the UK, you're part of the 244 family.

## Get Involved

Whether you're a student just arriving in the UK or a seasoned professional looking to give back to the community, there's a place for you at 244 Club. Join us at our next event, follow us on social media, or reach out directly — we'd love to hear from you.
