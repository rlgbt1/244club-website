# About 244 Club

244 Club was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to create a home away from home for Angolan students in the United Kingdom.

## Our Story

The idea for 244 Club was born out of a shared experience — the excitement of studying abroad mixed with the longing for the familiar sounds, flavours, and warmth of Angola. A group of Angolan students across several UK universities came together to build something that didn't yet exist: a nationwide network connecting every Angolan student in the country.

The name **244** comes from Angola's international dialling code. It represents the call home, the connection to our roots, and the signal that no matter where we are, we carry Angola with us.

## Our Mission

To unite, empower, and celebrate the Angolan student community across the United Kingdom through cultural events, professional development, and mutual support.

## Our Values

- **Ubuntu** — "I am because we are." Community comes first.
- **Resiliência** — The resilience that defines the Angolan spirit.
- **Orgulho** — Pride in our heritage, our language, and our culture.
- **Crescimento** — Growth through education, networking, and shared experience.

## The Palanca Negra

Our logo features the **Giant Sable Antelope** (*Hippotragus niger variani*), known as the **Palanca Negra** — Angola's national symbol and one of the rarest animals on Earth. Found only in Angola, the Palanca Negra represents resilience, beauty, and national pride. It is a fitting emblem for a community that carries the spirit of Angola wherever it goes.

## Committee

Our committee is made up of passionate students from universities across the UK, working together to organise events, build partnerships, and grow the 244 community.
