# Get in Touch

We'd love to hear from you — whether you're looking to join 244 Club, propose an event, collaborate, or simply say hello.

## Follow Us

Stay up to date with our latest events, announcements, and community stories by following us on social media.

## Join the Community

244 Club is open to all Angolan students and those with a connection to Angola studying in the United Kingdom. There are no membership fees — just a shared love for our culture and community.

> Interested in joining the committee? Drop us a message and tell us a bit about yourself and what you'd like to contribute.
