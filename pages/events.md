# Events

From cultural celebrations to professional networking, our events bring the Angolan community together across the UK.

## How to Attend

Most of our events are free for members. Keep an eye on our Instagram for announcements, or check back here for the latest updates. Some events may require registration — links will be posted when available.

## Past Highlights

Our events have ranged from intimate study sessions and cultural film nights to large-scale celebrations for Angolan Independence Day. Each gathering is an opportunity to connect, celebrate, and grow together.

> Want to suggest an event or collaborate with us? Head to our contact page and let us know!
