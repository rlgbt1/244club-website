What is 244 Club?

More than
a dialling code.

244 is Angola's international prefix — the digits Angolans press when calling home from abroad. For us, it's something deeper: a symbol of identity, roots, and the invisible thread that connects us across distance.

We built 244 Club for every Angolan student and young professional navigating the UK — so you never have to choose between who you are and who you're becoming.
