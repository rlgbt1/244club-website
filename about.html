Who we are

More than
a dialling code.
A declaration.

244 is Angola's international calling prefix — the numbers pressed when calling home from abroad. For us it means something more permanent: identity, roots, and the invisible thread that connects us across distance and time.

"We built 244 Club so that Angolan students and young professionals in the UK would never have to choose between ambition and identity — between building their future and staying rooted in who they are."
— 244 Club
01

Why we exist

Moving abroad is exciting and hard in equal measure. You're building a career, a new life, new friendships — all while navigating a culture that doesn't always reflect yours back at you.

We created 244 Club to make that journey easier, richer, and far less lonely.

02

The name

+244 is Angola's international dialling code. It's the prefix that connects Angolans abroad to home — the digits that carry family, memory, and identity across thousands of miles.

When we chose the name 244 Club, we chose what it represents.

03

Social & Cultural

Dinners, meetups, cultural gatherings, and community events that bring Angolan students and professionals together in the UK. Spaces to connect, celebrate, and feel genuinely at home.

04

Professional & Development

Industry talks, networking sessions, and strategic partnerships that open doors. From our landmark McKinsey & Company event to one-on-one connections between members — we believe your network should reflect your ambition.

Mission

To create a trusted space where Angolan students and young professionals in the UK can connect, grow, and thrive — together.

Vision

A generation of Angolans abroad who are deeply connected — to each other, to their roots, and to the opportunities around them.
