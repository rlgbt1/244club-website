244 Club belongs to every Angolan in the diaspora — wherever you are in the world. When you join, you become part of a growing community with access to events, a network, cultural moments, and opportunities we build together.

Our in-person events and community space are currently based in the United Kingdom — global membership, UK-based gatherings for now.

Who is this for?

244 Club is for Angolans outside of Angola — wherever in the diaspora you are.

The Community

244 Club was built for all Angolans living outside of Angola. Our community is global — if you're Angolan in the diaspora, this is your space.

Students & Professionals

Undergraduate students, postgraduate students, and young professionals are all welcome. If you're Angolan and building your path abroad, you belong here.

In-Person Events

For now, our community events, socials, and professional evenings are based in the United Kingdom only. We are growing and plan to expand further.

Virtual Events

Our online talks, panels, and virtual sessions are open to any Angolan student or young professional worldwide who would benefit from attending.
